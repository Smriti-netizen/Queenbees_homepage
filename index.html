About Queenbees

The online women only hiring webplatform

#herPlace

In the post covid world,as the employment opportunities become scarce and so the labour in the cities.Both employeees and employers around the country are facing a huge lot of problem. But for the women employers and employees ,the situation become even worse,as they face more gender discrimination,assualts and ill-treament, from their employers as well as from their employs.

In order, this pandemic and the coming post-covid world, doesn't become a curse instead of a boon for all the working women, we develop this platform to help women employers search for women employees, and women employees get job at women employers,thus helping each other to grow.

We also have assualt hepline and help desk to help women in case of any assualt from their employers or fellow collegues, even for those not working in Queenbees job network.
